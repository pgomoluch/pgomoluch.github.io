Paweł Gomoluch

Hi, I'm Paweł. I am a Senior AI Scientist at BenevolentAI. Previously, I was an AI Fellow at GSK, where I worked on drug-target binding prediction and active learning.

I received my PhD from the Department of Computing, Imperial College London. My research there was focused on enhancing model-based combinatorial search using machine learning techniques. In particular, I was working on learning heuristic functions and search policies for classical planning.

I studied Computer Science at Imperial (MSc Advanced Computing) and the Silesian University of Technology in Gliwice, Poland (AEI).
