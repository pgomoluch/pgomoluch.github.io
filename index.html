Paweł Gomoluch

Hi, I'm Paweł. I'm an AI Fellow at GlaxoSmithKline, where I'm conducting research on machine learning applied to drug discovery. Previously, I was a PhD student at the Department of Computing, Imperial College London. My research there was focused on enhancing model-based combinatorial search using machine learning techniques. In particular, I was working on learning heuristic functions and search policies for classical planning.

I received MSc in Advanced Computing from Imperial College. Before that I completed a master's (mgr) and a bachelor's (inż.) degree in Computer Science at the Faculty of Automatic Control, Electronics and Computer Science of the Silesian Univerity of Technology in Gliwice, Poland.
